How to integrate Minted with Splitwise

  1. Register your application

    Go to the Splitwise Developer Portal and log in with your Splitwise account. Click on "Register your application".

  2. Fill in the application details

    Give your application a name (e.g. "Minted Home Server") and enter the Homepage URL (e.g. http://localhost:4200). The most important field is the "Callback URL" / Redirect URI.

    {{ splitwiseRedirectUri || 'http://localhost:4200/integrations/splitwise/callback' }}
  3. Copy your credentials

    Once the application is created, Splitwise will provide a Consumer Key (Client ID) and a Consumer Secret (Client Secret). Copy both of these values and paste them into the fields on this settings page.

  4. Enable the integration

    Save the Splitwise Configuration settings here by clicking "Save Splitwise Config". Then, any standard user can go to the Integrations Module in the sidebar to connect their own personal Splitwise account and link their contacts.

Note: Splitwise has severely locked down their OAuth application approval process. Your external app will be created in "sandbox" mode or requires an explicit review from their support team to function perfectly on public instances.
